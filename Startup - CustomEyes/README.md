# CustomEyes

**CustomEyes** is an early-stage startup focused on developing an AI-driven system for creating perfectly fitted eyeglasses through automated measurement and adjustment.

The idea originated from a common problem in optometry: glasses are often adjusted manually through trial and error, even though facial structures vary significantly between individuals. This can lead to discomfort, pressure points, and headaches for many glasses wearers.

CustomEyes aims to address this issue by combining **3D imaging, machine learning, and automated frame adjustment** to produce glasses that are tailored to each individual’s facial structure.

---

## Current Stage

This project is currently in the **research phase**.

At the moment, the primary work involves:
- Developing a technically feasible system architecture
- Reviewing existing research related to facial scanning, pressure distribution, and frame adjustment
- Exploring potential hardware and machine learning approaches
- Consulting with professors and researchers to evaluate possible technical implementations

Unlike many software startups, this concept requires the **development of new hardware systems in addition to software**, including sensing, mechanical actuation, and machine learning integration.

Because of this, progress is focused on **research, feasibility analysis, and technical exploration** rather than rapid product development.

---

## Concept Overview

The proposed system consists of two main components:

### 1. Facial Scanning and Fit Analysis
A compact scanning system designed for optometry clinics that captures a detailed 3D model of a patient’s face in under one minute. 

Machine learning models would analyze:
- Facial asymmetries
- Pressure distribution points
- Frame alignment relative to the head

From this data, the system calculates the **optimal frame adjustment required for comfort and stability**.

### 2. Automated Frame Adjustment
A second machine would apply **precise mechanical and thermal adjustments** to a chosen pair of glasses.

Potential mechanisms include:
- Localized heat application for frame shaping
- Motor-driven fine mechanical adjustments
- Automated alignment based on the computed fit model

The goal is to perform the entire adjustment process **in minutes with significantly greater precision than manual adjustments**.

## Technology Areas Being Explored

- 3D facial scanning and depth sensing  
- Computer vision and facial mapping  
- Machine learning for fit optimization  
- Reinforcement learning using user feedback  
- Precision mechanical actuation  
- Heat-based frame reshaping systems  

These technologies would work together to create a **repeatable and scalable system for custom eyewear fitting**.

## Market Context

Globally, billions of people rely on prescription glasses, and discomfort from poor frame fit is a widespread issue.

The initial target market for CustomEyes would likely include:

- Optometry practices  
- Eyewear retailers  
- Medical clinics  
- Research institutions studying vision or ergonomics

The system could potentially be sold as specialized equipment for clinics and eyewear providers.
