# Leadership

This folder contains materials related to my leadership roles and professional development experiences. These projects highlight experience in team coordination, event planning, mentorship, and collaboration within student organizations.

---

## Folder Contents

### CaribSA
Materials and projects developed for the Caribbean Student Association (CaribSA) while serving as Programs Director. This includes General Body Meeting (GBM) presentations, over which I had full creative direction, as well as programming initiatives aimed at fostering cultural engagement and strengthening community on campus.


### FLEG
Documentation and materials related to the **Freshman Leadership Engineering Group (FLEG)**. This includes organizational initiatives, leadership development materials, and planning resources for major events.

Examples include:
- Executive Vice President leadership materials  
- Corporate Formal documentation
